Travels Around the Globe...

One of my favorite ways to enjoy life is to travel the world. I view traveling as a way to step into the lives of those vastly different from me, both reinforcing the elements of my life that I take for granted and revealing new ways of living. So far, I have travelled internationally to Europe and China, but I hope to expand my adventures soon.
